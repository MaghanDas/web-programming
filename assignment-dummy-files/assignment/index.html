Budapest Metro — Build & Operate

Design four metro lines and score as many points as possible.

Tip: Click Draw Card to get a station letter, then click an endpoint and a matching station to build.

Game rules (short)

Each round you build a single metro line (M1–M4). Click Draw Card to reveal a station type (A/B/C/D/Joker). Then extend your current line by one straight segment (horizontal, vertical, or 45°) from an endpoint to a station matching the card. Rounds end after 8 cards. After 4 rounds the game ends and the score is calculated.

  • Segments may touch other lines only at stations.
  • Segments cannot pass through other stations or cross in open space.
  • Deák tér (Joker) acts as any letter.
